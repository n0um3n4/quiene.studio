About the studio

Built on friendship and bad puns

quiene.studio is a two–person game studio created by a Mexican–Taiwanese duo who have been best friends since college. We grew up in different cultures, but bonded over the same things: games, stories, and the feeling of “wait, is this idea too weird?”

We don’t chase trends. We chase that specific feeling when a game makes you laugh, think, and care about a character made of 12 polygons and a single sound effect.

Remote-first dev PC + console (someday) Indie at heart Story + mechanics = ♥

Our goals are simple:

  • Make games that feel personal and a bit strange.
  • Experiment with narrative, culture, and humor.
  • Stay small enough to still be having fun.

The name

Why “quiene.studio”?

If you speak Spanish, you probably already hear it: “¿Quién estudió?”

The pun

quiene.studio sounds like “¿Quién estudió?”, which means “Who studied?” in Spanish.

It’s our way of saying: we take our craft seriously, but also not too seriously. We’re always learning, always experimenting, and slightly confused most of the time — in a good way.
